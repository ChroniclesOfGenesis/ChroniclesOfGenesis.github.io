1. Agree to COC

You must read and agree to the Chronicles of Genesis Code of Conduct and register on our Discord before becoming an official member of the COG family.

2. No Cheating

Cheating, hacking, game and/or system tampering will get you dismissed from COG. Please read and follow all Xbox Live Terms, conditions,, and regulations and abide by thhem. This is a dismissible offence

3. No Discrimination

Discrimination of any type in COG is not acceptable. All members should respect one another regardless of their rank, age, race, gender, etc... You are representing cog, so do the same and respect gamers outside of COG. This rule applies under any circumstance where you are representing COG including practices, open lobbies, xbox voice/text messages, COG Discord chats or any medium offered through our website. This is a dismissible offence.

4. No Harassment

Harassment in any form, sexual or otherwise, is absolutely unacceptable. If you violate this section, or the previous, prepare to be judged by higher-ups who have absolutely no tolerance for this type of behaviour. This is a dismissible offence.

5. Age

All members in cog must be 14 years of age or older and have a working mic. No exceptions!!!!

6. Responsibility

All members in cog are responsible for themselves and their actions. Higher ups within cog are not responsible for members who violate the rules. However members who violate the rules will be reviewed by members within cog leadership.

7. Relationships

Members connected with a personal relationship outside of cog need to make sure their relationship stays outside of cog. Members are not allowed to use personal relationships to alter cog business, rank, promotions, events, or any related activities. This is a dismissible offense.

8. Feedback & Complaints

COG has an open door policy to present any compliments or complaints. Please use the chain of command when you need to express these things. You should never go over your superiors' heads. Please respect your leaders. Disrespecting a higher up is a dismissible offense.

9. Taunting

Any taunting action over a beaten or dead player is prohibited while in practice, tournaments, or custom games. However it is allowed in matchmaking as long as you are not taunting a fellow cog member. This is a dismissible offence.

10. Unbiased COC

The code of conduct is unbiased; all members are accountable to the code of conduct. Encouraging another member to violate any rule of the code of conduct including this one will result in a demotion or dismissal.

11. Loyalty

We ask for 100% loyalty from all members in cog. You can only be in cog only and no other clan or community on xbl. Remember that we are a family and should have each other's back.

12. Family

Never abandon another cog member. To leave in the middle of a game (without warning and good reason) is what we consider abandoning a cog member. We are a family and we will win or lose together.

13. Meeting

Members should attend at least one meeting per week and at least two practices a week. By taking part in our weekly activities we will become stronger as a unit, and a family.

14. Responses

If you are called to a party by a higher-ranking member please respond to that person asap. We don’t expect you to quit out of a game for us, but please message us to know you heard our call. Ignoring a higher-up’s party invites repeatedly is a dismissible offence.

15. Modding

Modified (modded) controllers are not allowed during practice or tournaments. If you use them in practice, you will be kicked out of practice for a week. If you use them in a tournament, your team is automatically disqualified! Repeated violations of this article is a dismissible offence.

16. Personal Information

COG does not require the sharing of any personal information such as your name, address, phone number, pictures, etc. With any COG members. Any and all members who engage in the sharing of any personal information do so at their own risk.

17. Profile Information

Remember to make sure that your profile information is available so that we may verify you as fast as possible.

18. Fun

The most important rule: Always Remember To Have Fun!
